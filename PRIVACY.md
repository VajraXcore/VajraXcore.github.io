# VajraX — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2026**

## Overview

VajraX is built on a simple principle: **your data is yours.**

We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal
information or usage data of any kind.

---

## Permissions

VajraX requests only two permissions:

**INTERNET**
Required for live network tools:
- Network Scanner (host discovery)
- DNS Lookup (record resolution)
- Ping & Connectivity (ICMP reachability)
- SSL Inspector (TLS certificate inspection)

These tools send packets to addresses YOU specify.
No data is sent to VajraX servers — because there are none.

**ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE**
Required to detect connectivity status and show
appropriate offline/online banners in the UI.

No other permissions are requested or used.

---

## Third-Party Services

VajraX uses **zero third-party services.**

- No advertising SDKs
- No analytics platforms (no Firebase, no Mixpanel)
- No crash reporting services (no Crashlytics, no Sentry)
- No social login or account systems
- No cloud sync or backup services

---

## Data Storage

All data generated by VajraX (calculations, exports)
is stored **locally on your device only.**

PDF, CSV, and JSON exports are saved to your device
storage and are never transmitted anywhere.

---

## Network Activity

VajraX only makes network connections when you
explicitly use a live network tool and provide
a target address. All connections are:

- Initiated by you
- Directed to addresses you specify
- Never logged or stored
- Never sent to VajraX
